Peter Spiegel - new Secretary General of the Club of Budapest International since March 2002 - the Team and future

 

In March 2002 Peter Spiegel took on the post as Secretary General of the Club of Budapest International. Both of his predecessors have taken on new responsibilities within the Club: Dr. Thomas Druyen, leader of the Club of Budapest, Germany now is president of the Supervisory Board, supporting the Secretary General and the President in searching for patrons for exemplary future projects and other of the club's activities. Dr. Christopher Laszlo represents the Club in the newly established Planetary Ethics Alliance, a network of leading organisations, lobbying a new global ethos.

Peter Spiegel new Secretary General of the
international Club of Budapest

We warmly welcome Peter Spiegel as the new Secretary General of the international Club of Budapest. The head office has been relocated from Washington to Stuttgart. We wish Peter Spiegel, that his many competencies and his experience may add to the furthering of the Clubs world wide. We are looking forward to close co-operation with Mr Spiegel. Find his details about his visions in his Newsletter.

Thomas Druyen new Chairman of the Supervisory Board of the international Club of Budapest

Dr. Thomas Druyen has been nominated the chairman of the new Supervisory Board of the internationalen Club of Budapest. Together with a few outstanding personalities he will assist General Secretary Peter Spiegel and President Ervin Laszlo in winning reputable patrons for the various projects of the Club of Budapest.
